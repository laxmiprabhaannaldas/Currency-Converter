KUWAIT

The Indian Rupee is the currency in India (IN, IND). The Kuwaiti Dinar is the currency in Kuwait (KW, KWT). The Kuwaiti Dinar is also known as the New Kuwaiti Dinar. The symbol for INR can be written Rs, and IRs. The symbol for KWD can be written KD. The Indian Rupee is divided into 100 paise. The Kuwaiti Dinar is divided into 1000 fils. The exchange rate for the Indian Rupee was last updated on August 26, 2019 from The International Monetary Fund. The exchange rate for the Kuwaiti Dinar was last updated on August 26, 2019 from The International Monetary Fund. The INR conversion factor has 6 significant digits. The KWD conversion factor has 6 significant digits. Large amounts of Rupees are expressed in lakh rupees or crore rupees. A Lakh Rupee is one hundred thousand rupees and a crore rupee is ten million rupees.
