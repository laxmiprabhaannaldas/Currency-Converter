SWITZERLAND

The Swiss Franc is the currency in Switzerland (CH, CHE), and Liechtenstein (LI, LIE). The Indian Rupee is the currency in India (IN, IND). The symbol for CHF can be written SwF, and SFr. The symbol for INR can be written Rs, and IRs. The Swiss Franc is divided into 100 rappen (centimes). The Indian Rupee is divided into 100 paise. The exchange rate for the Swiss Franc was last updated on August 26, 2019 from The International Monetary Fund. The exchange rate for the Indian Rupee was last updated on August 26, 2019 from The International Monetary Fund. The CHF conversion factor has 6 significant digits. The INR conversion factor has 6 significant digits. Large amounts of Rupees are expressed in lakh rupees or crore rupees. A Lakh Rupee is one hundred thousand rupees and a crore rupee is ten million rupees.
