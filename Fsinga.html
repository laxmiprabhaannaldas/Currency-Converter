SINGAPORE

The Indian Rupee is the currency in India (IN, IND). The Singapore Dollar is the currency in Singapore (SG, SGP). The symbol for INR can be written Rs, and IRs. The symbol for SGD can be written S$, and SGD$. The Indian Rupee is divided into 100 paise. The Singapore Dollar is divided into 100 cents. The exchange rate for the Indian Rupee was last updated on August 26, 2019 from The International Monetary Fund. The exchange rate for the Singapore Dollar was last updated on August 26, 2019 from The International Monetary Fund. The INR conversion factor has 6 significant digits. The SGD conversion factor has 6 significant digits. Large amounts of Rupees are expressed in lakh rupees or crore rupees. A Lakh Rupee is one hundred thousand rupees and a crore rupee is ten million rupees.
